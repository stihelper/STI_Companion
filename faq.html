Need a hand? Don't worry! Here is a list of common questions and answers to help you find your way. You’re definitely not the only one figuring things out — it's normal to need help.

Common Questions

▶Q: How do I pay my tuition?

Payments can be made at the cashier's office or through the student portal.

▶Q: Where can I access my schedule?

Your class schedule is available on the student portal.

▶Q: What if I have issues with my subjects?

You can visit your department office or contact your adviser.

▶Q: Can I still enroll if I missed the deadline?

Late enrollment is subject to approval from the Registrar.

▶Q: How do I reset my student portal password?

Click “Forgot Password” on the login page and follow the steps.
